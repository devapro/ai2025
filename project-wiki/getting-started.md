# Getting Started Guide

## Welcome!

Welcome to our platform! This guide will help you get up and running in just a few minutes.

## Create Your Account

### Step 1: Sign Up

1. Visit the homepage and click "Sign Up"
2. Enter your email address and create a strong password
3. Accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
4. Click "Create Account"
5. Check your email and verify your account

Your password should be at least 8 characters long and include:
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character (recommended)

### Step 2: Choose Your Plan

After verifying your email, you'll be prompted to choose a plan:
- Start with the Free Plan to explore features
- Upgrade anytime as your needs grow

You can change your plan later from Settings > Billing.

### Step 3: Complete Your Profile

1. Add your name and profile picture
2. Set your timezone for accurate timestamps
3. Choose your notification preferences
4. Add team members if you're on a paid plan

## Your First Project

### Creating a Project

Projects help you organize your documents and collaborate with team members.

To create a project:
1. Click "New Project" from the dashboard
2. Enter a project name and description
3. Choose privacy settings (Private or Team)
4. Click "Create"

You can create unlimited projects on all plans.

### Uploading Documents

To add documents to your project:
1. Open your project
2. Click "Upload" or drag-and-drop files
3. Supported formats: PDF, DOCX, TXT, MD, XLSX, PPT
4. Maximum file size: 100 MB per file (500 MB on Pro/Enterprise)

Documents are automatically indexed for search within minutes.

### Organizing with Folders

Keep your project organized with folders:
1. Click "New Folder" in your project
2. Name your folder
3. Drag documents into folders
4. Create subfolders for deeper organization

You can move documents between folders anytime.

## Collaborating with Your Team

### Inviting Team Members

To invite team members:
1. Go to Settings > Team
2. Click "Invite Member"
3. Enter their email address
4. Choose their role:
   - **Viewer**: Can view documents only
   - **Editor**: Can view and edit documents
   - **Admin**: Full access including settings
5. Click "Send Invitation"

Team members will receive an email invitation with instructions.

### Sharing Documents

Share individual documents without adding someone to your team:
1. Open the document
2. Click "Share"
3. Enter email addresses
4. Set permissions and expiration (optional)
5. Click "Send"

Shared links can be revoked anytime from the document settings.

### Real-Time Collaboration

Work on documents together in real-time:
- See who's viewing or editing (cursor indicators)
- Changes sync automatically
- Comment and discuss inline
- Track version history

No need to save - everything is auto-saved!

## Using the Search Feature

### Basic Search

Our powerful search helps you find information quickly:
1. Use the search bar at the top
2. Enter keywords or phrases
3. Results appear as you type
4. Click any result to open the document

### Advanced Search

Use search operators for precise results:
- `"exact phrase"` - Find exact matches
- `title:keyword` - Search in titles only
- `type:pdf` - Filter by file type
- `date:2024` - Filter by year
- `tag:important` - Search by tag

Example: `"quarterly report" type:pdf date:2024`

### Semantic Search

Our AI-powered semantic search understands context:
- Search by concept, not just keywords
- Ask questions in natural language
- Find related content automatically

Try: "How do I improve team productivity?"

## Keyboard Shortcuts

Speed up your workflow with shortcuts:
- `Ctrl/Cmd + N` - New document
- `Ctrl/Cmd + O` - Open document
- `Ctrl/Cmd + S` - Save (auto-save is on by default)
- `Ctrl/Cmd + F` - Search in document
- `Ctrl/Cmd + /` - Show all shortcuts
