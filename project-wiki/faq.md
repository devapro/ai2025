# Frequently Asked Questions

## Account Management

### How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:
1. Go to the login page
2. Click on "Forgot Password?" link
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Check your email for a password reset link (it may take 5-10 minutes)
5. Click the link and create a new password
6. Your password must be at least 8 characters and include uppercase, lowercase, and numbers

If you don't receive the reset email within 15 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

### How do I change my email address?

To change your email address:
1. Log into your account
2. Navigate to Settings > Profile
3. Click "Change Email"
4. Enter your new email address
5. Verify the change by clicking the confirmation link sent to your new email

Note: You'll need to verify your new email before the change takes effect.

### How do I update my billing information?

To update billing information:
1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Click "Update Payment Method"
3. Enter your new credit card or payment details
4. Click "Save Changes"

All billing information is encrypted and secure. We support Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal.

## Features and Usage

### What are the main features of the platform?

Our platform includes:
- **Document Management**: Upload, organize, and search documents
- **Collaboration Tools**: Share files and collaborate in real-time
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Track usage and performance metrics
- **API Access**: Integrate with third-party applications
- **Advanced Search**: Find information quickly with semantic search
- **Version Control**: Track changes and restore previous versions

### How do I share documents with team members?

To share documents:
1. Open the document you want to share
2. Click the "Share" button in the top right
3. Enter the email addresses of team members
4. Set permissions (View, Edit, or Admin)
5. Click "Send Invitation"

Recipients will receive an email with access to the document.

### Can I integrate the platform with other tools?

Yes! We offer integrations with:
- Slack (for notifications)
- Google Drive (for file sync)
- Zapier (for workflow automation)
- Microsoft Teams (for collaboration)
- Salesforce (for CRM integration)

To set up integrations, go to Settings > Integrations and follow the setup wizard.

### What are the API rate limits?

API rate limits depend on your plan:
- Free Plan: 100 requests per hour
- Basic Plan: 1,000 requests per hour
- Professional Plan: 10,000 requests per hour
- Enterprise Plan: Custom limits

If you exceed your limit, you'll receive a 429 error. Upgrade your plan for higher limits.

## Billing and Subscriptions

### How much does the service cost?

We offer several pricing tiers:
- **Free Plan**: $0/month - Basic features, 1 GB storage
- **Basic Plan**: $9.99/month - 50 GB storage, priority support
- **Professional Plan**: $29.99/month - 500 GB storage, advanced features
- **Enterprise Plan**: Custom pricing - Unlimited storage, dedicated support

Annual subscriptions receive a 20% discount.

### How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription:
1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Click "Manage Subscription"
3. Select "Cancel Subscription"
4. Confirm your cancellation

Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing period. No refunds for partial months.

### Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days, contact support for a full refund.

After 30 days, we don't provide refunds for partial months, but you can cancel anytime to avoid future charges.

### How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

To change your plan:
1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Click "Change Plan"
3. Select your new plan
4. Confirm the change

Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

## Security and Privacy

### Is my data secure?

Yes, we take security seriously:
- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256
- We perform regular security audits
- We comply with SOC 2 and GDPR standards
- Multi-factor authentication is available

### Do you sell my data to third parties?

No, we never sell user data to third parties. Your data is yours, and we only use it to provide our services. See our Privacy Policy for details.

### How do I enable two-factor authentication?

To enable 2FA:
1. Go to Settings > Security
2. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
4. Enter the verification code
5. Save your backup codes in a safe place

We strongly recommend enabling 2FA for enhanced account security.
